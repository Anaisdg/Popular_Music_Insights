Geographical Music Insights

A closer look at musical preferences across 15 cities — filtered by artist genre, race, and gender — based on Spotify streaming frequency.

How to interact:

  • Click on any city to see its demographic breakdown, top streamed track, and top 5 streamed artists.
  • Check off filters on the right to visualize how many artists fall into each category.

When we visualize artist popularity in the context of physical location & demographics, we can observe influences on musical preference!
